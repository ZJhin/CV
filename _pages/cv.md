## Research Profile
PhD researcher specialising in Southern Ocean and Antarctic sea-ice processes, with a strong focus on sea-ice-driven water-mass transformation (WMT), freshwater redistribution, and ocean ventilation and carbon uptake in CMIP6 climate models. Extensive experience in large-scale model diagnostics, inter-model comparison, and Python-based analysis on HPC systems. Actively preparing multiple journal manuscripts and seeking post-PhD research opportunities in polar climate and ocean-ice interactions.
